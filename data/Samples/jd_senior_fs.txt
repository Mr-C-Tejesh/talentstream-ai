# Job Description: Senior Full-Stack Engineer (AI focus)

**Location**: Remote
**Experience**: 5+ Years

## Overview
We are looking for a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to join our AI Platform team. You will be responsible for building scalable web applications and integrating advanced AI models into our core product.

## Key Responsibilities
- Design and develop robust, scalable backend services using Python (FastAPI/Django).
- Build intuitive and responsive user interfaces with React and Tailwind CSS.
- Integrate LLMs (Large Language Models) like GPT-4, Claude, and Llama 3 into production workflows.
- Optimize application performance and ensure high availability.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define and implement new features.

## Requirements
- 5+ years of experience in full-stack development.
- Deep proficiency in Python and JavaScript/TypeScript.
- Experience with modern frontend frameworks (React, Next.js).
- Strong understanding of RESTful APIs and asynchronous programming.
- Familiarity with vector databases (Pinecone, Milvus) and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).
- Proven track record of delivering high-quality software in a fast-paced environment.

## Preferred Qualifications
- Contributions to open-source project.
- Experience with multi-agent systems (CrewAI, LangGraph).
- Knowledge of DevOps practices and cloud platforms (AWS, GCP).
